About me

With a background in mechatronics and robotics, I design without separating form from production reality.

Market research → idea generation → sketching → CAD → engineering → prototyping → verification

Constraints stay visible

Production pressure, a material limit, an unverified assumption. All of it stays visible in the project, not tucked away.

Designed as a system

Not a single object, but its maintenance, its use cycle and the user journey, considered together.

Manufacturing shapes form

Sheet bending, injection molding, 3D printing. The chosen method is part of the design decision, not a note added later.

Verifiable results

Pool test, jury evaluation, real use. What can be shown matters, not the claim.
