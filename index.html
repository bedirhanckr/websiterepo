A designer who works with manufacturing in mind.

From underwater robotics to steel facade systems, I'm an industrial designer who speaks the same language as engineering teams. I never separate the form decision from production reality.

FocusProduct Design · Mechanism · Manufacturing
HighlightTEKNOFEST, Design & Presentation Award
BackgroundMechatronics · Robotics · Steel Structure Design

I don't hide constraints

Production pressure, a material limit, an unverified assumption, all of it stays visible in the project.

I think in systems

Not a single object, but its maintenance, its use cycle and the user journey, designed together.

Manufacturing shapes form

Sheet bending, injection molding, 3D printing, the chosen method is part of the design decision, not a note added later.

I look for verifiable results

Pool test, jury evaluation, real use, the result matters, not the claim.
